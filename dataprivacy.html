Contact Form / Waitlist Sign-Up

If you contact us via the contact form or sign up for our waitlist, the information you provide–including your email address–will be stored by us for the purpose of processing your request and for any follow-up questions. This data will not be shared without your consent.

The processing of the data entered into the form is based exclusively on your consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR). You can revoke this consent at any time. A simple email to us is sufficient.

Your data will be stored only as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose of the processing or as long as we are legally required to retain it.

Cookies

Our website uses only technically necessary cookies required for the operation of the website. These cookies do not harm your device and do not contain viruses. No personal data is stored in these cookies. Therefore, no separate consent is required.

Additional Tools and Plugins

We do not use any analytics tools, newsletters, or additional plugins that collect or evaluate personal data.

Your Rights

You have the right at any time to request information, free of charge, about your stored personal data, its origin and recipients, and the purpose of the data processing. You also have the right to request the correction, blocking, or deletion of this data, provided no legal retention obligations exist.

Right to File a Complaint with Supervisory Authorities

If you believe your data has been processed unlawfully, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the responsible supervisory authority. For us, this is the Data Protection Commissioner of the State of Hesse (Hessen) in Germany.

Data Protection Officer

No data protection officer has been appointed, as this is not legally required for private individuals under current GDPR regulations.
