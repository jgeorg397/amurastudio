About Us

Amura Studio is a founder-led family office built on long-term ownership, operational excellence, and disciplined capital allocation.

Founded by [TBU] and [TBU], the entrepreneurs behind [TBU], Amura Studio draws on deep experience in building and scaling global businesses. That operating background shapes our approach: we invest with conviction, act as owners, and focus on durable value creation rather than short-term outcomes.

Our activities are structured across three interconnected pillars.

Through our Builder & Ventures Lab, we partner with founders at the earliest stages, building companies hands-on from idea to scale. Our Real Assets platform focuses on tangible, long-term investments such as land, forestry, and real estate, prioritizing substance, resilience, and sustainability. Complementing both is our Capital Engine, which systematically allocates capital across markets to preserve, grow, and compound wealth over time.

The name Amura refers to the bow of a ship — the part that cuts through waves and sets the direction. It reflects how we operate: leading from the front, navigating complexity with clarity, and maintaining a clear course through changing environments.

Amura Studio invests its own capital, thinks in decades, and operates independently of fund cycles. This long-term, entrepreneurial mindset allows us to move decisively, stay aligned with our partners, and build value that lasts.

Builder & Ventures Lab

We build and back companies from the very beginning.

Amura Studio partners with founders at the earliest stages — from idea to scale — combining capital, operational execution, and long-term ownership. Our venture building activity spans a broad range of business models, with a particular focus on D2C. In addition, we selectively invest in early-stage technologies, with a strong emphasis on AI, Space, and Defense, where we see long-term relevance and structural tailwinds. Through hands-on execution and rapid prototyping, we turn strong ideas into focused, durable businesses.

Real Assets

We invest in tangible assets with enduring value.

Our Real Assets platform focuses on the acquisition, development, and long-term stewardship of physical assets. This includes investments in land and forestry, as well as residential and commercial real estate and selected asset-backed opportunities. We deploy patient capital, take an active ownership approach, and manage assets with a long-term horizon.

Capital Engine

We compound capital through disciplined allocation.

The Capital Engine forms the financial backbone of Amura Studio. It combines diversified, liquid strategies with selective alternatives and systematic approaches, designed to preserve capital, manage risk, and generate reliable returns. This engine provides stability, flexibility, and the firepower to support long-term ownership and new opportunities.
